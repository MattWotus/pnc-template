GOVERNMENT AFFAIRS

ELECTION CYCLE TOTALS

A special message from Bill Demchak,

Over the past few years, we have worked hard to increase PNC’s engagement in the policy and political processes to ensure that our views are represented at all levels of government. This engagement includes providing leadership on matters of public policy, supporting elected officials and candidates for office whose views are consistent with the company’s, and building relationships with our elected representatives to ensure that our voices are heard when important decisions are being made. We have had a number of legislative successes in the past year or so in a tough political environment, which is a testament to our engagement strategy.

The PNC Political Action Committee (PNC PAC) is a vital part of this process. By pooling our voluntary contributions, we are able to support candidates who are willing to work with us to achieve our policy goals, and we are able to have meaningful conversations with them about the issues that are important to us.

Contributing to the PAC is easy and even a small contribution can make a difference when it comes to supporting candidates who are willing to work on common sense solutions that impact our business. And for those who want to do more, we are continuing our Chairman’s Club program.

Thank you for considering a contribution to the PNC PAC. Please know that participation in the PNC PAC is strictly voluntary. It is your choice.

If you have any questions or need additional information, please contact Shawn Smith on our Government Affairs team at 202-835-4539 or shawn.d.smith@pnc.com.

Sincerely,

Bill Demchak
Chairman
President and Chief Executive Officer
